Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. At PDF Masters, we've built our platform with privacy-first principles.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Key Privacy Promise: All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device, and we never see their contents. No uploads, no server storage, complete privacy.

1. Introduction

Welcome to PDF Masters. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

PDF Masters is built on the principle of privacy-first design. Unlike other PDF tools, we process all files directly in your browser, ensuring maximum security and privacy.

3. Information We Automatically Collect

Like most websites, we collect some anonymous technical information to improve our services:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and language settings for compatibility
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and screen resolution for optimization
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, time spent, and tools used (anonymous and aggregated)
  • IP Address: Anonymized for security and geographic analysis (country-level only)

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our services and user experience. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and session duration (anonymized)
  • Referral sources and traffic patterns
  • Geographic location (country-level only)
  • Device and browser information for compatibility

4.2 Google AdSense

To support our free services, we display advertisements through Google AdSense:

  • AdSense may use cookies to serve relevant advertisements
  • Collects non-personal information for ad targeting
  • Uses IP addresses to determine approximate location for regional ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential Functionality: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with our website
  • Advertising: Display relevant advertisements (via third parties)
  • Performance: Optimize website speed and responsiveness

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • Client-Side Processing: Your files stay on your device
  • Regular Security Updates: Continuous monitoring and updates
  • Secure Infrastructure: Protected hosting environment
  • No Data Retention: We don't store processed files

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@pdfmasters.com.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your information:

  • Right to Know: What information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of any information we hold
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data collection where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 10.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Provide a summary of changes for significant updates

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
